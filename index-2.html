Premium Add-On Services

An elevated standard
for practices that demand it

For clinics and networks that require more than standard courier service, Velora offers a suite of premium protocols tailored to your specific clinical, operational, and patient-facing needs.

White-Glove Handling Protocols
Elevated care standards for fragile, high-value, or critical specimens requiring exceptional handling from pickup through delivery.
Temperature-Control Awareness
Informed transport of temperature-sensitive specimens and medications using cooler systems and careful monitoring protocols.
Discreet, Unmarked Delivery
No visible branding or identifiers on the vehicle or packaging — ideal for sensitive patient situations and privacy-first practices.

Client-Specific Handling SOPs
Custom standard operating procedures developed around your practice's exact workflow, packaging requirements, and preferred communication style. Velora adapts to you — not the other way around.
